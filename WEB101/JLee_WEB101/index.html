Event Schedule

The Catacombs.

Entrance: The Watcher

LEVEL 5 This strange creature is roaming the Catacombs to add powerful adventurers to its collection.

Floor I: Bonzo

LEVEL 15 Involved in his dark arts due to his parents' insistence. Originally worked as a Circus clown.

Floor II: Scarf

LEVEL 25 First of his class. His teacher said he will do "great things".

Floor III: The Professor

LEVEL 30 Despite his great technique, he failed the Masters exam three times. Works from 8 to 5. Cares about his students.

Floor IV: Thorn

LEVEL 50 Powerful Necromancer that specializes in animals. Calls himself a vegetarian, go figure.

Floor V: Livid

LEVEL 65 Strongly believes he will become the Lord one day. Subject of the mockeries, even from his disciples.

Floor VI: Sadan

LEVEL 80 Necromancy was always strong in his family. Says he once beat a Wither in a duel. Likes to brag.

Floor VII: The Wither King

LEVEL 150 Disciples of the Wither King. Inherited the Catacombs eons ago. Never defeated, feared by anything living AND dead.
